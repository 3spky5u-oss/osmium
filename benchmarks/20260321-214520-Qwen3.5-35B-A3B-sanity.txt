## 1. Core Concept and Prerequisites

### The Logic
Binary search works by repeatedly dividing the search interval in half. It relies on the assumption that the data is **sorted**.
1.  Compare the target value to the middle element of the array.
2.  If they match, the search is complete.
3.  If the target is less than the middle element, discard the right half and search the left.
4.  If the target is greater than the middle element, discard the left half and search the right.
5.  Repeat until the element is found or the interval is empty.
